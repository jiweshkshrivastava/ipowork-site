MB Platform Agreement
ipowork Merchant Banker Platform Agreement

1. Platform Access — ipowork grants you access to its deal flow platform upon SEBI Category I registration verification. Access is non-transferable and limited to your registered firm.

2. Success Fee — For any issuer mandated through ipowork, a success fee of 0.75%–1.00% of the gross IPO issue size is payable to ipowork upon stock exchange listing.

3. Anti-Poaching Clause — Any issuer whose profile you access through ipowork is a "Platform-Originated Issuer." You shall NOT engage, solicit or accept a mandate from any Platform-Originated Issuer outside of ipowork for a period of 36 months from the date of first profile access. Breach triggers liquidated damages equal to 0.75%–1.00% of the gross issue size plus 18% p.a. interest, recoverable under Indian Contract Act 1872 Section 74.

4. Confidentiality — All issuer information accessed through the platform is confidential. You shall not disclose issuer identity or financial data to any third party.

5. SEBI Compliance — You warrant that your firm holds a valid SEBI Category I Merchant Banker registration for the duration of platform access. You are solely responsible for all regulatory compliance in your IPO mandates.
