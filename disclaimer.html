Data Accuracy Statement

ipowork continuously validates and updates information from multiple reliable sources, including MCA21, Probe42, SEBI databases, credit rating agencies, EPFO, GST Network, and court records. While we strive to provide accurate, timely, and comprehensive research, the following limitations apply:

Source dependency: Information may be incorrect, incomplete, or outdated at the underlying source. ipowork reflects source data as received and cannot independently verify every data point.

Data lag: Financial statements, annual filings, and regulatory orders may be published with a delay of weeks to months after the underlying event. ipowork reports the most recently available filed data.

AI limitations: AI-assisted document analysis may occasionally misinterpret complex legal language or unusual financial structures. All AI outputs are reviewed by human analysts, but errors may occur.

No representation: No representation or warranty, express or implied, is made that any information on this platform is complete, accurate, current, or error-free. Information may change without notice.

Users remain solely responsible for independently verifying all material information before making any financial, legal, or investment decision.
