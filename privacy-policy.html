DPDP Act 2023 Compliance

This Privacy Policy is prepared in accordance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India). ipowork is committed to processing personal data lawfully, transparently, and only to the extent necessary for legitimate business purposes.

1
Who We Are

ipowork (operating as ipowork.com) is a capital markets research and intelligence platform serving institutional professionals in India. We are the Data Fiduciary for personal data collected through our platform.

Registered address: [ipowork registered address]
Data Protection Contact: privacy@ipowork.com

This policy applies to personal data collected from users of ipowork.com and related portals. It does not apply to data about companies, which is sourced from public registries and is not personal data within the meaning of the DPDP Act.

2
Personal Data We Collect

ipowork collects minimal personal data — only what is necessary to provide our research services and maintain our platform. We collect data in two ways: data you provide to us, and data generated by your use of our platform.

Category Data Collected How Collected Purpose
Account Data Name, professional email address, organisation name, designation Registration form / onboarding Account creation and access management
Usage Data Pages visited, reports accessed, search queries, session duration, browser type, IP address Automatically via server logs and cookies Platform improvement, security monitoring, abuse prevention
Communication Data Email content when you contact us, support requests Direct communication Responding to enquiries and providing support
Subscription Data Subscription tier, billing name, payment reference (not card details — payments processed by third-party gateway) Subscription and billing process Service delivery and billing
Report Delivery Data Client firm name, logo URL provided by institutional users for co-branded reports Report admin portal White-label and co-branded report delivery to end clients

What we do not collect: ipowork does not collect sensitive personal data such as Aadhaar numbers, PAN, bank account details, biometric data, health information, or political opinions. We do not collect data from minors.

3

We use personal data only for the following purposes, each supported by a lawful basis under the DPDP Act 2023:

Purpose Lawful Basis
Providing access to the ipowork platform — account management, login, service delivery Consent / Contract performance
Delivering research reports — generating and delivering AIRA reports to institutional subscribers Contract performance
Platform security and fraud prevention — monitoring for abuse, unauthorised access, data breaches Legitimate interests
Service improvement — analysing usage patterns to improve platform features and research quality Legitimate interests

4
Company Data & Director Information

ipowork's primary research activity involves processing data about companies and corporate entities — not about individual consumers. This includes company financials, legal history, charge registers, GST compliance, and EPFO records sourced from MCA21, Probe42, and other public registries.

ipowork also processes publicly available information about company directors, KMPs, and promoters — including names, DIN numbers, designations, shareholding percentages, and appointment/cessation dates. This information is:

  • Sourced exclusively from public registries (MCA21, Probe42, BSE/NSE disclosures)
  • Processed for legitimate capital markets research and institutional due diligence
  • Not used for any purpose unrelated to professional corporate intelligence
  • Retained only as long as necessary for research and audit purposes

This processing is conducted under the legitimate interests ground of the DPDP Act 2023, as the information relates to individuals in their professional capacity as corporate fiduciaries and is sourced from public disclosures mandated by law.

5
Sharing of Personal Data

ipowork does not sell personal data to any third party. We share personal data only in the following limited circumstances:

Recipient What is shared Why
Supabase (database infrastructure) Report data, user account data Platform storage and database operations. Supabase is bound by data processing agreements.
Cloudflare (network infrastructure) Traffic data, IP addresses Content delivery, security, and DDoS protection.
Anthropic (AI processing) Document content uploaded for analysis AI-assisted document intelligence. Documents processed via Anthropic's API under their data processing terms. No personal consumer data is sent — only company documents.
Probe42 (data provider) Company CIN / search queries Structured company data retrieval. Query data is processed under Probe42's terms.
Regulatory authorities As legally required Compliance with applicable Indian laws, court orders, or regulatory requirements.
6
Data Retention

ipowork retains personal data only as long as necessary for the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law.

Data Type Retention Period
Account data (name, email, organisation) Duration of active subscription + 12 months after account closure
Research reports and AIRA data 5 years (for audit and institutional record purposes)
Usage logs and access records 12 months
Communication and support records 3 years
Billing records 7 years (as required by Indian accounting and tax laws)

Upon expiry of the applicable retention period, personal data is securely deleted or anonymised. You may request earlier deletion of your personal data — see Section 7 (Your Rights) below.

7
Your Rights Under the DPDP Act 2023

As a Data Principal under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

📋
Right to Access
Request a summary of the personal data ipowork holds about you and how it has been processed.
✏️
Right to Correction
Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data held by ipowork.
🗑️
Right to Erasure
Request deletion of your personal data, subject to applicable legal retention requirements.
🚫
Right to Withdraw Consent
Withdraw consent for processing at any time where consent is the lawful basis. Withdrawal does not affect past processing.
📣
Right to Grievance Redressal
Lodge a complaint with ipowork's data protection contact. If unresolved, escalate to the Data Protection Board of India.
👤
Right of Nomination
Nominate another individual to exercise your data rights in the event of your death or incapacity (as provided under Section 14 of the DPDP Act).

To exercise any of these rights, write to privacy@ipowork.com. We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

8
Security Measures

ipowork implements appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These include:

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between users and ipowork is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher via Cloudflare
  • Access controls: Platform access is PIN and credential protected; admin functions require separate authentication
  • Database security: Supabase Row Level Security (RLS) policies ensure data isolation between users
  • Minimal data collection: We collect only the data necessary for stated purposes
  • Breach notification: In the event of a data breach likely to affect your rights, we will notify you and the Data Protection Board as required by the DPDP Act

No security system is completely impenetrable. In the unlikely event of a data breach, ipowork will notify affected users promptly and take all reasonable steps to mitigate harm.

9
Cookies

ipowork uses only essential cookies necessary for platform functionality — session management, authentication state, and security tokens. We do not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking cookies, or behavioural profiling cookies.

You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect your ability to use certain features of the platform.

ipowork does not display advertisements and does not allow any advertiser to track your behaviour through our platform.

10

ipowork may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or applicable law. Material changes will be notified to registered users by email or by a prominent notice on the platform at least 15 days before the change takes effect.

The date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last updated. Continued use of ipowork after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Privacy Questions & Requests

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, to exercise your data rights, or to lodge a grievance, contact our Data Protection Officer. We respond to all requests within 30 days.
