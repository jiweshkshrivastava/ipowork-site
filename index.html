📊 AIRA Score Bands

What Your Score Means

The AIRA Score maps directly to SEBI ICDR eligibility thresholds.

< 50
Not IPO Ready
Significant gaps in financials, governance or compliance. Remediation needed before approaching any MB.
50–64
SME with Gaps
Close to eligibility. 2–4 key gaps need fixing. Typically 12–18 months to reach SME IPO threshold.
65–77
SME IPO Eligible ✅
Meets NSE Emerge / BSE SME thresholds. Ready to approach merchant bankers and initiate DRHP.
78+
Main Board Eligible 🎯
Meets NSE / BSE Main Board standards. Strong governance, clean compliance and solid financials.
