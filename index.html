⚡ The IPO Readiness Problem

Why 80% of SMEs Are
Not IPO Ready

Most promoters approach merchant bankers 2–3 years too early — or arrive with problems that take years to fix. AIRA tells you exactly where you stand.

📊
Weak Financial Reporting
Poor MIS, delayed audits, qualified audit reports, revenue recognition issues — these red-flag every merchant banker instantly. SEBI requires 3 years of clean, restated financials.
Affects 73% of SME IPO aspirants
⚖️
Compliance Complexity
ROC filings overdue, secretarial compliance gaps, SEBI ICDR violations, missing statutory registers. Any litigation becomes an IPO red flag that triggers SEBI queries.
Top reason for DRHP rejection
🏢
Governance Weaknesses
No independent directors, family-dominated board, no audit committee, RPTs not at arm's-length, promoter pledge — investors penalise governance gaps with 20–40% valuation discount.
Average 3-year fix timeline
📈
No IPO Roadmap
No structured timeline, unclear when to convert to public company, no pre-IPO capital plan, no merchant banker engagement strategy. Promoters waste years without a clear path.
