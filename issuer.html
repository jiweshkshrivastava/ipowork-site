Top 10 Reasons SME IPOs Get Rejected by SEBI
Avoid these mistakes · Common SEBI observations · How to fix them
▾
① Promoter Loan Irregularities
Unsecured loans from promoters or related parties not properly documented. Fix: formal loan agreements, board resolutions, proper interest rates
② Related Party Transactions
Undisclosed or under-priced transactions with promoter entities. Fix: all RPTs at arm's length, board approval, proper disclosure
③ Weak Board Composition
Insufficient independent directors or non-compliance with Companies Act. Fix: minimum 1/3 independent directors, proper committee formation
④ Cash Transactions in P&L
High cash sales above ₹2 lakh without proper trail. Fix: shift all transactions to banking channels at least 2 years before IPO
⑤ Incomplete MCA Filings
Missing AOC-4 or MGT-7 filings with MCA. Fix: file all pending forms immediately, maintain 100% MCA compliance going forward
⑥ GST Irregularities
Mismatch between GST returns and audited revenue. Fix: reconcile GSTR-1 vs GSTR-3B vs P&L for last 3 years, resolve all notices
