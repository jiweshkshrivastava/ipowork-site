What Does an SME IPO Cost in India?

A typical SME IPO on NSE Emerge or BSE SME costs between ₹1.5 Cr and ₹4.5 Cr for a ₹30–60 Cr issue. The biggest single expense is the merchant banker (lead manager) fee, which typically ranges from 3–5% of issue size for SMEs.
